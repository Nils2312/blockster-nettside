EXPERIENCES BUILT FOR DISCOVERY AND WONDER

Blockster was founded by Nils Kristian Bjøro in 2020 with a passion for creating immersive Minecraft experiences. Each world is carefully handcrafted, from landscapes and structures to gameplay and story. Designed to be explored, these worlds reflect a singular vision shaped by creativity, attention to detail, and a deep love for the game.

Over time, Blockster grew, with help from others contributing custom music and voice acting to bring the stories to life.

BUILT FOR IMMERSION

Each world is built from scratch and designed to feel alive. With original music and professional voice actors, every story becomes immersive and emotional. This is more than a map, it's a full experience.

CRAFTED TO STAND OUT

Not just built. Shaped. Every map is crafted to feel like a complete game of its own, with deep lore, layered storytelling, and characters that matter. The goal is not just to entertain. It is to create an experience that pulls you in and makes you care about the world you're in.

From eerie soundscapes to meaningful quests, every detail is placed with purpose. You’re not just exploring, you’re stepping into a living, breathing story where your actions leave a mark.
